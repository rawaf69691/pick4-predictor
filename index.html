Neural Strategy Guide

Balanced Neural
A hybrid approach that balances Frequency (how often a number appears) with Recency (how lately it appeared). It looks for statistical clusters that tend to appear together without leaning too heavily on outliers.
Aggressive Pattern
Prioritizes Momentum and short-term streaks. This strategy analyzes the most recent 10-14 draws to catch "hot" digits that are currently clumping, betting that the immediate trend will persist for another cycle.
Conservative Math
Based on Regression to the Mean and Gap Analysis. It identifies "cold" numbers that are statistically overdue based on the law of large numbers, predicting the digits most likely to break their long-term absence.
